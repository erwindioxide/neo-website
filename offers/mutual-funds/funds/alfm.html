ALFM Mutual Funds

A wholly owned subsidiary of the Bank of the Philippine Islands

The ALFM Mutual Funds are open-end investment companies registered with, and regulated by, the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). Originally established in 1997 as Ayala Life Filipino Income Fund, Inc. (now ALFM Peso Bond Fund), the ALFM Family of Mutual Funds has grown to 7 different mutual funds catering to the different investment needs of clients. It is the first and only fund portfolio that offers a true multi-currency stable of mutual funds.

As of May 5,2021, the ALFM Mutual Funds have the largest market share in the mutual funds industry in the Philippines.
